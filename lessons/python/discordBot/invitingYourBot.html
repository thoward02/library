Inviting your bot

Why we invite our bot

There are many reasons we invite our bot.

It allows us to test the functionality of our bot before we make the choice to release it to our server or other servers.

We can also invite other people to our server in order to gain feedback.

Inviting our bot

If you already know how to invite a discord bot you can just skip over this page

On the developers page we left open earlier there should be a button named "invite your bot". Click this and you will be able to copy your link into your browser.

When we load the page there will be a series of instructions that you will have to follow in order to invite your bot. You should have already created a server to test your bot in. If you do not know how to create a discord server you can visit our other tutorials or find an online tutorial on the subject.

When you've invited the bot you will be able to see its profile in your discord server. For now the bot will stay offline until we start programming it.
